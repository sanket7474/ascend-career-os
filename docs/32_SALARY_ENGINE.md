# Purpose

The Finance Engine is designed to help users manage their financial life alongside their career growth.

Unlike traditional expense trackers, ASCEND should answer:

* Can I afford my dream trip?
* Can I buy a house?
* Can I buy a car?
* Am I saving enough?
* How long until financial freedom?
* What is my net worth trend?

The Finance Engine should act as a personal financial dashboard.

# Final Goal

The Finance Engine should help users make informed financial decisions by connecting income, expenses, investments, liabilities, and life goals into a single unified dashboard.

Rather than simply tracking money, it should provide a clear roadmap toward financial stability and long-term wealth creation while supporting personal aspirations such as travel, home ownership, and financial independence.
