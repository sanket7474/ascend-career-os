# Purpose

The Career Engine is the heart of ASCEND.

It transforms scattered activities such as learning, projects, interview preparation, and consistency into measurable career progress.

Instead of tracking only study hours, ASCEND should answer one question:

> **"How close am I to becoming the engineer I want to be?"**

---

# Vision

Every user should wake up and immediately understand:

* What level they are
* What skills are weak
* What to study today
* What projects to build
* What salary they can realistically target
* What skills are blocking the next promotion

The Career Engine provides this insight.

Every study session, project, interview, and habit should contribute toward a single objective:

**Becoming a better software engineer through consistent, measurable progress.**
