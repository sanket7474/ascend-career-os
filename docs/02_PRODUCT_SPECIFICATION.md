# Product Overview

ASCEND is a Career Operating System.

It combines:

* Career Planning
* Daily Planning
* Learning Management
* Finance
* Analytics
* Interview Preparation
* Projects
* AI Guidance

into one application.

The objective is to make every engineer intentional about career growth.
