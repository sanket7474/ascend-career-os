# Purpose

The Interview Tracker is designed to systematically prepare users for software engineering interviews.

Rather than randomly solving questions, users should follow a structured interview preparation roadmap that measures readiness across multiple dimensions.

The Interview Tracker should answer:

* Am I interview ready?
* Which topics are weak?
* Which companies can I target?
* How many questions have I solved?
* What should I revise today?

# Final Goal

The Interview Tracker should become a complete interview preparation system that combines technical preparation, behavioral practice, mock interviews, application tracking, and revision scheduling into a single unified experience.

The objective is not just to solve questions.

The objective is to maximize the probability of receiving high-quality software engineering offers through structured, measurable, and consistent preparation.
